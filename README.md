Safe, Sound and Seen: NourishHer
Technology to empower women's wellbeing in shelters. 

Overview:
In shelter environments, the challenge is not a lack of knowledge. Rather, it is the difficulty of turning that knowledge into action under real-world constraints. Women already understand the importance of nutrition and wellbeing, but limited food access, shared spaces, financial pressure, and unpredictable routines make consistency nearly impossible.

NourishHer is designed to bridge this gap.

We present an interactive, accessible digital experience that transforms nutritional knowledge into practical, achievable daily actions—supporting both physical health (Feeding the Body) and a sense of joy, agency, and identity (Feeding the Soul).

The Solution:

Our platform is a low-barrier, intuitive website tailored specifically for shelter environments, where access, time, and privacy are limited. We want this to go far behind a nutritional tool to a holistic well-being tool. 
At its core, the tool enables users to:

Generate nutritious, filling, and cost-efficient recipes based on realistic constraints such as limited ingredients, shared kitchens, and tight budgets.
Explore an interactive virtual kitchen that makes learning about food engaging, visual, and approachable.
Understand macronutrients in a simple, meaningful way. 
Reduce decision fatigue and help women gain more self-control and reclaim their identity.

What makes us stand out:

NourishHer truly understands the underlying difficulties with living a shelter and the following constraints. 
Every aspect of the platform is intentionally built around the realities outlined by Community Women’s Initiative (CWI):
- Respects privacy: requires no user account or login credentials.
- Accesible - Accommodates for over 13 languages, reducing reliance on English fluency. Visually driven and engaging.
- Works on shared devices - ideal for shelter computers or common-use tablets.
- Quick interactions - we understand that not everyone has the time or desire to request an interactive experience with their recipes. Provides alternative, efficient, easy to read format with visual engagement.


Extended Impact:

- Many women live with their children in the shelters. We help make this a more inclusive platform by using game-based learning experiences around food and nutrition.
- Incorporates curiosity and intuitive eating habits.
- Balances mental wellbeing and physical nourishment in challenging environments. 
- Promotes a sense of community through shared meals, comfort food and restoring self-agency.
