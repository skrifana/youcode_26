About NourishHer

Built for YouCode 2026 in partnership with the Community Women's Initiative (CWI), which supports women in shelters across BC's Lower Mainland.

Food is more than fuel — it's comfort, culture, and identity. NourishHer helps shelter staff and residents turn whatever ingredients are available into nourishing, culturally aware meals. No account, no sign-up, no wasted time.

It addresses the Feeding the Body pillar of CWI's wellbeing framework — translating nutrition knowledge into actionable guidance under the real constraints of shelter life.
