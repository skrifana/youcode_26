About

What is YouCode?

YouCode is a compassionate tool designed to help women in transition shelters across British Columbia discover nourishing recipes tailored to their shelter's facilities.

How it works

Search for your shelter by name or city. Based on whether your shelter has a shared kitchen, we suggest recipes you can realistically make — full meals if you have a kitchen, or simple no-cook options if you don't.

Our mission

Good food is dignity. We believe every woman in a shelter deserves access to simple, culturally-aware, and nutritious meal ideas regardless of their circumstances.
